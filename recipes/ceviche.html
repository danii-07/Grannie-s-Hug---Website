Ceviche

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo of fish of your choice, diced into small cubes
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 sweet red peppers, finely chopped
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, very finely chopped
  • The juice of approximately 15 lemons (enough to cover the fish)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, place the thawed fish and cover it completely with the lemon juice. Stir well to ensure all the fish is in contact with the juice.
  2. Add the chopped onion and pepper to the fish, mixing carefully to distribute the ingredients evenly.
  3. Verify that the lemon juice completely covers the fish. If necessary, add more lemon juice.
  4. Add the chopped cilantro and salt to taste. Stir again until everything is well incorporated.
  5. Let the mixture rest in the refrigerator for two hours so that the fish marinates and cooks in the lemon juice.
  6. Serve the ceviche accompanied by soda crackers, fried green plantains, and sauces to taste.

Servings

5-7 servings
