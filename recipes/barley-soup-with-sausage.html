Barley Sausages

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked barley
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Salt, to taste
  • ¼ cup flour (all-purpose or chickpea)
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat and sauté the onion until soft, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  2. Stir in the cooked barley, cumin, paprika, black pepper, and salt. Mix thoroughly and cook for 3-4 minutes, allowing the spices to toast lightly.
  3. Remove the pan from heat and let the mixture cool slightly. Then, transfer it to a food processor or blender and pulse until the mixture holds together. You may need to add a little bit of water if it seems too dry.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a bowl, then stir in the flour and breadcrumbs until the mixture binds together and can be shaped into sausage-like forms. You can adjust the consistency with a bit more flour if needed.
  5. Shape the mixture into 6-8 sausages, depending on the desired size. Heat a little olive oil in the pan and cook the sausages over medium heat for about 5-6 minutes per side, until golden and crisp.
  6. Remove the sausages from the pan and serve hot. Garnish with fresh parsley. Enjoy!

Servings

4 servings
