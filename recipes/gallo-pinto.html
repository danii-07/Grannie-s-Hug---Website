Gallo Pinto

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked day-old white rice
  • 1.5 cups of cooked day-old black or red beans (or 1 can)
  • 1.5 cups bean broth or water
  • 0.5 cup yellow onion (roughly 1/2 medium onion, chopped)
  • 0.5 cup red pepper (roughly 1/2 medium pepper, chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons Salsa Lizano
  • Sea salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil or olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, pepper, and salt.
  3. Lightly cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Then add the beans with broth and toss with the chopped vegetables and oil.
  5. Add the rice (cooked) to the skillet.
  6. And toss until well combined with the beans and spices.
  7. Add the cilantro and lightly toss.
  8. Heat thoroughly and add the Salsa Lizano.
  9. Mix again and then serve- preferably with fried eggs, warm corn tortillas and a hot cup of Costa Rican coffee or agua dulce.

Servings

4-6 servings
