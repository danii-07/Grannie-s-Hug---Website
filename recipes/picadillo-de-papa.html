Picadillo de papa

Ingredients

  • 8 medium-large potatoes (white and yellow)
  • 1/2 kilo ground beef
  • Butter
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Onion
  • Chile pepper
  • Cilantro
  • Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Annatto (achiote)

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into cubes.
  2. Cook the potatoes in water (without salt) until the cubes soften.
  3. Finely chop the onion, chile pepper, and cilantro and add them to the ground beef.
  4. Add seasoning and Lizano sauce (or another similar sauce) to the ground beef and mix with your hand until everything is well blended.
  5. Cook the ground beef in a skillet.
  6. Once the potatoes are cooked, drain the water, and in the same pot, add a cube of butter, a small splash of oil, and a little annatto.
  7. Incorporate the ground beef and stir over very low heat to prevent the potatoes from breaking apart.
