Gazpacho

Ingredients

  • 900 grams tomatoes, quartered
  • 400 grams Persian cucumbers, peeled and chopped, plus more chopped for serving
  • 75 grams red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, roughly chopped
  • 30 milliliters red wine vinegar or sherry vinegar
  • 120 milliliters water
  • 80 milliliters extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for pan and garnish
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 thick slices country bread
  • 6 cherry tomatoes, halved, for topping (optional)
  • 30 milliliters thinly sliced basil

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes, cucumbers, pepper, garlic, vinegar, and water in the bowl of a food processor or blender. Blend until smooth, then add oil and blend to combine. Taste and season with salt, pepper, and more vinegar if needed. Cover and refrigerate until chilled.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium heat, add enough oil to coat the bottom of the pan. Add bread and cook until golden and crisp on the bottom, 1-2 minutes. Flip bread to other side and cook until crisp, 1-2 minutes more. Remove from pan and let cool.
  3. To serve, divide soup among bowls and top with cucumber, tomatoes (if using), basil, and a drizzle of oil.

Servings

6-8 servings

For 6 servings
